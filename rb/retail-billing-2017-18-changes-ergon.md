Retail Billing : 2017-18 Changes (Ergon)
========================================

**NEW Charges**

CAC Tariffs

As noted in our TSS and 2016-17 Pricing Proposal we are proceeding with the introduction of an excess reactive power charge (excess kVAr charge) for our CAC network tariffs in 2017-18.

Charged as: $/excess kVAr per month

  

Code: CAPACITY\_ERGON\_CAC_EXCESSREACTIVE
