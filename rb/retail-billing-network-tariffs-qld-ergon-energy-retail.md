### Demand

**Any Time Maximum** **Demand (ATMD):**

Code: DEMAND\_ERGON\_ANYTIME

Is the maximum half hourly demand for a customer that occurs at any time within a specified period.

The Peak Demand calculation uses the greater of the maximum kVA demand in any single half hour at any time, during the peak demand period in each summer month.

The Off-Peak Demand calculation uses the greater of the maximum kVA demand in any single half hour at any time, during each non-summer month and the minimum chargeable demand.

**Capacity charges:**

Code: CAPACITY_ERGON

Tariff 66: Irrigation (Transitional) uses a two-step demand charge ($/kW). Block 1 is for the first 7.5kW of motor capacity used for irrigation pumping and Block 2 is the remaining. These are Annual Fixed Charges.

Switching Tariffs
-----------------

Customers essentially assign themselves to a tariff class by selecting the service that they require. Therefore, written notification of the tariff class is not provided by Ergon Energy to the retailer. Changing of tariffs can be done by contacting Ergon directly by phone. No info is available around forms to be completed or expected period of time for tariff change.

If a retailer raises an objection to a tariff class assignment or reassignment for Alternative Control Services, the matter is reviewed and if required, escalated to the  
Manager Regulatory Determination and Pricing for reassessment.
