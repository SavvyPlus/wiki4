**Tariff 7000 Residential Demand:**

Code: DEMAND\_ENERGEX\_RESI

Maximum kilowatt demand measured as a single peak over a 30 minute period during **peak** charging window (between 4pm and 8pm on workdays during billing period).

Workdays are weekdays but exclude government public holidays.
