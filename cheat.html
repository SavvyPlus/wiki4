Let's do this

In here, you will find a repository of cheatsheets I've collected through the years to do presentations more engaging and to write better content. We usually put some constraints in ourself by thinking if is technical doesn't have to be fun, it's quite the opposite, nobody likes to be in a presentation with excellent content and a boring presenter.

Beside probably one of your best friends "Oski" worked with INCSUB as a board advisor in a tech and M&A capacity, as well as founded Neuli and Cymplar. Incsub receives over 4 million visits per month and has produced or host millions of blog post around a variety of subject, internally they've created a cheat sheet, and I copied it. Neuli and Cymplar reach were mostly publications on other sites and free resources which are still today being used and replicated by thousands of people. What I've learned : One of the most critical skills a founder must have is the capacity to prepare, present and communicate, 9 out of 10 this is by reading and writing, nobody is born with it, it's all practice hard work and sacrifice. Or have Cheatsheet :-), so let's do the second one.
